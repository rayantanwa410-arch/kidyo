MEET THE FOUNDER

Leading with Vision

Meet the founder:

Lorine founded *Kid Your Confidence* with a belief shaped by her own upbringing: every child deserves someone who believes. Even during difficult moments for her family, her parents never let her forget about her purpose, potential, and worth. The constant love and encouragement helped shape who she is today, and who she’s becoming. But Lorine noticed that many children don’t grow up with that same support. Some spend their days in pain in hospitals, while others move between homes. Many face circumstances that make them feel invisible, unworthy, or uncertain of their place in the world. Realizing how easily confidence can fade before a child ever has the chance to discover their strengths, Lorine knew she couldn’t ignore the problem. *Kid Your Confidence* exists to bring hope to children who need it most. Through confidence kits, mentorship, and messages of encouragement, we remind kids that they matter, their dreams are real, and their future is worth believing in. Every child has a light inside them. *Kid Your Confidence* is here to help them see it—and keep it shining—so they can feel confident today and grow into leaders tomorrow.
