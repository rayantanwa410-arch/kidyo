About Us

Our Founder

Hi, I'm Lorine. I started Kid Your Confidence because I grew up with something every child deserves: someone who believed in me. Even when life was hard for my family, my parents never stopped reminding me about my purpose and capabilities. That love and belief shaped who I am today — and who I'll become tomorrow. But I know many children don't have that opening. Some spend their days in hospitals, while others bounce between homes. Some face challenges that make them feel invisible, unworthy, or useless. It hurts to think about how many of these children lose confidence before ever getting the chance to discover who they could be. I couldn't ignore that. Kid Your Confidence exists to bring hope to the kids who need it most — the ones who deserve a spark, a smile, a reminder that they matter. Through confidence kits, birthday surprises, and messages of encouragement, we show kids that their dreams are real and that their future is worth believing in. Every child has a light inside them. We’re here to help them see it — and keep it shining. We're here to make them feel confident today and grow into leaders tomorrow.
